Our Mission

North Carolina has become one of the few southern states without a ban on abortion. NCT4AA understands such non-resident patients looking to get an abortion safely will travel to NC to proceed legally. Thus, we donate all proceeds to the Carolina Abortion Fund, a nonprofit dedicated to funding abortions for anyone in need in the Carolinas.
